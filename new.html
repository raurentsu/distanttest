Who we are?

The circus was created in 1885 by aleksei dikiy a Russian acrobat born in 1858 in Moscow.
Arriving in the United States in the 1880s, he decided to teach the circus arts and set up his own troupe.

After a brilliant career in the city of chicago , he decided to leave the circus in 1931 and give it to his
two children irina and vladimir who returned to Europe after the second world war.
From this years, the wild circus has been a true pioneer and recreated the art of the circus by innovating througth
the modern techniques and technology.
Traveling in all countries, our circus is famous in the world.
